Non-White Kitchens

  • By: Admin /
  • Projects /
  • 3 Comments

If there was one defining design trend of the 2010s, it was the all-white kitchen. But in 2020, designers predict there'll be a shift toward spaces that go beyond just white. Like to see more natural wood cabinets, islands, and shelves - all of which allow homeowners to keep a bright and airy vibe without restricting themselves to all-white-everything.

One of the best ways to make your kitchen and bathroom stand out in the coming year is to incorporate a lively backsplash. 2020 is all about bold patterned tiles and colors. If you have someone over and they’re seemingly staring out into the distance, it’s not you. It’s the kitchen.

Non-White Kitchens are also just as stunning and casual feeling as the classic White kitchen look! Typically kitchens, without the right treatment, can be cold and clinical spaces, but the warmth of the beautiful oak in kitchen counteracts this. we have a round-up of inspiring non-white kitchens for those of you craving a different look. For a more dramatic look that feels warm and inviting, consider a rich green or blue.

The overall look is elegant but relaxed - its sociable layout allows for practical family living.
